Frequently Asked

Questions

Explore frequently asked questions about our veneer products, including applications, types, durability, and maintenance. For any additional inquiries, don’t hesitate to reach out to our team for expert advice!

We provide a wide selection of veneer types, including natural wood veneer, engineered veneer, and reconstituted veneer. Each type has unique characteristics, allowing you to choose based on aesthetic preferences and durability needs.

Veneer is highly versatile and can be applied to most flat surfaces, including plywood, MDF, and particle boards. It’s ideal for cabinetry, doors, wall panels, and furniture. Our team can guide you on surface suitability for your project.

Our veneers are crafted for long-lasting beauty and durability when maintained properly. For areas with high wear, such as flooring or countertops, we recommend using high-quality veneers with protective finishes to enhance resilience.

Veneer surfaces should be cleaned gently using a soft, damp cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive materials that could damage the finish. Regular dusting and cleaning will help maintain its appearance over time.
